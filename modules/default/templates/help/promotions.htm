Help File: Site Promotions [ X ]
Site Promotions are very powerful methods of increasing your orders and order sizes. There are three basic site promotions avilable. Each promotion is based on dates for its "Live" period.

You can have all the promotions turning live at the same time, but only one will actually be used. It is recommended that you do NOT have more than one live at a time.

If you wish to have all promotions turned OFF, set each date into the past (ie before today). This will insure that the promotions will not be displayed.

Promotions
  • Flat Discount The flat discount gives out a percentage based on the shopping cart total. If the cart total is set to £100.00 and the percentage is 10%, customers whos shopping cart meets and exceeds £100.00 will recieve a 10% discount on their products.


  • Gift Certificates The Gift Certificate builder gives you the ability to create a gift certificate and provide a code to your customers that they can use online for their purchases.


  • Gift Wrapping Gift Wrapping is available to your customers as they check out. Your customers have the option of choosing whether or not they would like to have an individual gift wrap added to their order (providing you have the gift wrapping options configured).


  • Discount Codes Discounts allows you to provide individuals or groups with discount codes to use when placing an order. Towards the end of the checkout, if a discount code is provided, the cart will take the percentage off of the total order and modify the order total.


  • Discount Ranges Discounts based on ranges are for example orders from £100.00 - £199.99 recieve a 5% discount, from £200.00 - £299.99 recieve a 10% discount and orders over £300.00 recieve a 15% discount.


  • Free Shipping If a cart total is over the specified amount, the customer recieves FREE shipping (Default shipping only).


  • Member Points Member points give the user the opportunity to purchase items from your store and build up points. The points have a currency value set by the administrator, so the more points built up the more credit the customer has available to spend.

    • Member Point Value Calculation to give a point value. If a customer has 2,000 points, and the point value is set to .03, the customer then has £60.00 worth of points. (2000 X .03 = 60).


    • Member Point Calculation Calculating value for each unit (£1) purchased. If it is set to 5, then for every £1.00 spent, the customer recieves 5 points (£1.00 = 5 Points).
