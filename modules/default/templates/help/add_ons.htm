Help File: Gift Wrapping [ X ]

Product Add Ons are availble to your customers as they check out. Your customers have the option of choosing wether or not they would like to have an individual add on added to their order (providing you have any gift wrapping options configured).

The editor builds add on options, with a small thumbnail image (if available), short text and an additional price (if applicable).

The additional price will be multiplied by the quantity for each item.

During the checkout, customers can choose from different add ons per line item in the shopping basket.
