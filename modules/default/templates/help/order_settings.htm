Help File: Order Settings [ X ]

  • Postage Title - The name of your default postage setting, for display in your shopping basket.
  • Order Email Address - The Email address that all order information is sent to.
  • Info Email Address - The Email address info requests are sent to (can be the same as Order Email).
  • Wireless Email Address - If you can recieve wireless emails (PDA, mobile phone etc.), enter your wireless email address here.
  • Accept Credit Cards - If accepting credit cards, which credit card types your shop will offer.
