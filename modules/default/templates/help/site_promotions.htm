Help File: Site Promotions

Site Promotions are very powerful methods of increasing your orders and order sizes. There are three basic site promotions avilable with your system. Each promotion is based on dates for its "Live" period.

You can have all the promotions turning live at the same time, but only one will actually be used. It is recommended that you do NOT have more than one live at a time.

If you wish to have all promotions turned OFF, set each date into the past (ie before today). This will insure that the promotions will not be displayed.

Promotions
  • Flat Discount The flat discount gives out a percentage based on the shopping cart total. If the carttotal is set to £100.00 and the percentage is 10%, customers whos shopping cart meets and exceeds £100.00 will recieve a 10% discount on their products.


  • Discount Ranges Discounts based on ranges are for example orders from £100.00 - £199.99 recieve a 5% discount, from £200.00 - £299.99 recieve a 10% discount and orders over £300.00 recieve a 15% discount.


  • Free Shipping If a cart total is over the specified amount, the customer recieves FREE shipping (Default shipping only).
