Help File: Gift Wrapping [ X ]

Gift Wrapping is availble to your customers as they check out. Your customers have the option of choosing whether or not they would like to have an individual gift wrap added to their order (providing you have the gift wrapping options configured).

The editor builds the gift wrap, with a small thumbnail image (if available), short text and an additional price (if applicable).

The additional price will be multiplied by the quantity for each item.

During the checkout, customers can choose from different gift wrap per item in the shopping basket.
