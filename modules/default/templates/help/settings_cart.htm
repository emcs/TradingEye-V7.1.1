Help File: Cart Settings [ X ]
  • Order Email Address - Email address order information is sent to.


  • Info Email Address - Email address info requests are sent to (can be same as Order Email).


  • Wireless Email Adress - If you can recieve wireless emails, put your wireless email address in here to have


  • Alternate Shipping - Check to enable alternate shipping (other than billing address).


  • Gift Wrapping - Check to turn on gift wrapping (you must define gift wraps in gift wrapping module).


  • Email List Signup - If you wish to allow customers to signup for your email list.


  • Payment Types - Define which payment methods you offer.


  • Accept Credit Cards - If accepting credit cards, which credit card types do you offer.


  • RealTime Process - If accepting credit cards or checks and want to process in realtime, select your processor. If your processor is not listed, select other and open up /cart/checkout_process.cfm to modify the realtime code to add your processor.


  • Default Shipping Title - IMPORTANT - Type in your default shipping method title here for display in the shopping cart.
