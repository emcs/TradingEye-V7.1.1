Help File: Suppliers [ X ]

The Suppliers module allows you to add, edit, view and delete the names and details of suppliers, manufacturers and companies in your database. This list allows you to associate the correct suppliers or manufacturers with each of your products.

Add New Supplier
This link allows you to create a new supplier in your database.

Search
The suppliers database can be searched by either keyword or the company name.

Contact
The supplier's email address will be displayed as a text link in this column.

Edit
Click the Edit link to view and / or alter the appropriate supplier's details.

Delete
The "Delete" link allows you to remove a supplier's details from your database. This will not affect any products.
