Help File: Feature Settings [ X ]

  • Stock Control - Stock Control is an all or nothing feature. If you choose this feature, it will track stock levels for all of your products, options and choices.
  • Customer Reviews - The default templates that are included with Tradingeye have a custom tag that offers reviews for customers to review and give opinions on each product. If you are logged in as an administrator, you can delete inappropriate reviews.
  • Wish List - Wish lists allow customers to build up a 'wish list' of products that they want and then email friends and family the list.
  • Member PointsMember points give the user the opportunity to purchase items from your store and build up points. The points have a currency value set by the administrator, so the more points built up the more credit the customer has available to spend.Change member points value from promotions section. click here promotions.
