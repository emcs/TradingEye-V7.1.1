Help File: Gift Certificates [ X ]

The Gift Certificate builder gives you the ability to create a gift certificate and provide a code to your customers that they can use online for their purchases.

Gift Certificates are like cash, if you have a £25.00 gift certificate, and the order is £35.00 (total), then the customer only has to pay £10.00 out of their own pocket. As well if the order is £10.00 (total) then the customer still has £15.00 available.

It is recommended that you do not delete any gift certificates after they have been used, this will benefit for customer lookups and reporting.
