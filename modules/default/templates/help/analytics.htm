Help File: Google Analytics [ X ]

To activate Google Analytics you must firstly have a google account. When you have activated your analytics account, simply paste the unique tracking code into the admin and hit the save button. After around 24 hours, your analytics account will have begun to store and assess your data.

It is important that you use the secure analytics code for your pages, otherwise your visitors may encounter an error! This occurs when your secure page loads in data from a non-secure webpage (like the normal google analytics code) - You can Google's article on the topic for further help
