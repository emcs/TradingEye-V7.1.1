Help File: Settings [ X ]
Settings are for modifying the various pieces and parts of Tradingeye Shop. You can change the colours, directories, text areas, headers, footers, features and cart settings through this module.

Text Areas
There are many customizable areas that Tradingeye Shop lets you manage from this area. You can modify the text or the HTML within these areas.

Company Information This is your company information that displays throughout the site. It also has all of your contact information as well.

Cart Settings Setup your cart information, email address, payment types, credit cards, realtime processor.
